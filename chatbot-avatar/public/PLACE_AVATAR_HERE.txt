Place your portrait image here as: avatar.jpg

The image shown in the chat (the portrait with dark hair and turtleneck) should be saved to:
  chatbot-avatar/public/avatar.jpg

Supported formats: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .webp
Recommended size: at least 420×420 px, square crop works best.

After placing the image, run:
  npm install
  npm start
